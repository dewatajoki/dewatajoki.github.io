Samsara Living Museum

Visit Samsara Museum In The Village Of Jungutan and Find The Cycle Toward Perfection

Samsara is one of the beliefs of Hindu, particularly in the Island of God. It is about the stages of life, characterized by various ceremonies and traditional rituals that lead to the belief in the task of living as humans in repeated births to perfection. All experiences in the journey of the Samsara Museum are best experienced in the spacious front field which provides a room for interactions between visitors. Along with pleasant memories of the museum's atmosphere that can be taken home along with unique artistic handicraft.
